Frequently Asked Questions

How does photo recognition work?

Open the app, tap "Take a Photo" on the home screen, and point your camera at the problem. Keep your phone steady and make sure the text is clear and well-lit.

Why doesn't HeySolver just give me the answer?

HeySolver is designed to help you truly understand problems, not just copy answers. It guides you step-by-step through the solving process with key questions and hints, building your independent thinking skills.

Can I ask follow-up questions?

Yes! After receiving a study guide, tap "Chat" to start a follow-up conversation with AI. You can ask up to 10 rounds of questions per problem for deeper understanding.

What subjects are supported?

HeySolver supports Math, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, History, Geography, Computer Science, and more. AI automatically detects the subject from your question.

What languages does HeySolver support?

The app interface is available in English, Korean (한국어), Japanese (日本語), and Simplified Chinese (简体中文). AI can respond in the same language as your question, or you can set a preferred response language.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open iPhone "Settings" → Tap your Apple ID at the top → "Subscriptions" → Find "HeySolver" → Tap "Cancel Subscription".

Will I lose my data?

Your learning data is saved locally on your device. If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is also backed up to iCloud and can be restored on a new device.
