Privacy Policy

HeySolver · Last updated: February 12, 2026

Introduction

HeySolver is an AI-powered learning assistant that helps students understand problems through guided questioning, step-by-step explanations, and follow-up conversations. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Our Privacy Commitment

HeySolver follows a "data minimization" principle. We only process data necessary to deliver our core features, and we take strict measures to protect your personal privacy.

Data Collection & Processing

To provide our core features, we process the following data:

  • Account Information: We collect your email address (if you choose to share it) through Apple Sign In or Google Sign In, solely to securely sync your account across devices.
  • Problem Images: Photos you take of problems are used for question recognition and analysis. We do not store your original images on our servers.
  • Problem Text: Text you enter is used to generate study guides. We do not store your original text on our servers.
We do not collect your location, contacts, or other sensitive personal information.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI-guided study explanations and learning guidance
  • Save your study history and favorites locally on your device
  • Verify your account identity for data sync

Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

To deliver our core features, we use the following third-party services:

  • AI Service (Google Gemini):
    • Provider: Google
    • Data transmitted: Problem images and text you submit
    • Purpose: Question recognition, study guide generation, follow-up chat
    • Data protection: All transmissions use HTTPS encryption. Data is used for real-time processing only and is not used for advertising or model training (see Google AI Terms)
  • Authentication (Supabase):
    • Purpose: Secure user authentication and account management
    • Only essential account information is stored
  • iCloud (Apple):
    • Purpose: Optional cross-device data sync
    • Data is stored in your private iCloud container
    • You can disable iCloud Sync at any time in app settings
  • Payment (Apple):
    • All subscription and purchase transactions are handled exclusively by Apple
    • We do not collect or access any of your financial information

When Your Data Is Shared

Your data is sent to AI services for processing only in the following scenarios:

  • When using "Photo Recognition": your problem photo
  • When using "Type Question": your problem text
  • When using "Follow-up Chat": your chat messages and the context of the current problem

Data Security

  • Transmission Security: All network communications use HTTPS encryption to ensure data privacy and integrity during transit.
  • Storage Security: Your original images and study data are stored locally on your device or in your private iCloud container.
  • Privacy Assurance: We do not use your data for targeted advertising, nor do we share your data with any third party for model training or secondary use.

Your Rights

  • View and delete your data within the app
  • Delete your account at any time — all data will be permanently removed
  • Disable iCloud Sync to keep data only on your device

Children's Privacy

HeySolver is designed for students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Policy Updates

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify you through the app.
